Encompassing serenity, Kerala is a charming South Indian destination that should be in every traveller’s bucket list.
Adorned with the emerald backwaters, sprawling and lush tea estates, pristine hill stations, and azure Arabian Sea
Kerala tourism boasts numerous tourist places with which you will fall in love instantly. In North Kerala, districts like Kasaragod, Kannur, Wayanad, Kozhikode, and Malappuram have many attractions for the tourists.
Wayanad offers a lovely hill station holiday whereas Kozhikode offers a great beach holiday and an opportunity of bird watching.
